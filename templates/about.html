    • Why RideCloud?

  • RideCloud is an easy-to-use, minimal, "lo-fi" ride logging application.

  • It won't tell you if you have the fastest time on your local hillclimb.

  • It also won't tell potential thieves where to find your beloved bicycle and when it's likely to be there.


    • RideCloud is Open Source!

  • RideCloud runs on a Python/Flask back-end. SQL Alchemy is used to interact with a SQLite database.

  • View the source code for RideCloud on GitHub.

  • For access to the RideCloud API, send an e-mail to the address listed above.
